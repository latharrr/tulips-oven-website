About Us

A Kitchen, Not a Factory

Tulip's Oven started in a Delhi kitchen with one oven, a stand mixer, and zero interest in cutting corners. We still bake that way — everything made after you order, boxed the same day. If a batch doesn't come out right, it doesn't go out at all.
